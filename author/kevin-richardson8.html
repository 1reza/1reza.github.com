Items of the Week: April 15, 2013

2013-04-22
  • You should blog even if you have no readers -- Two main reasons: writing makes you a better reader and writing makes you smarter.
  • Forecast -- A beautiful and incredibly functional weather web app that is very likely the (best) mobile weather application. The "installation process" for iOS is incredibly easy in Safari: you merely load forecast.io in Safari and follow the instructions.
  • Garmoshka -- Resize your browser window to "play" the accordion.
  • Song of GitHub -- Convert a person's GitHub commit history into a MIDI using MIDI.js. For an example, check out my Song of GitHub.
  • Nintendo 3DS XL -- Although I greatly enjoyed the regular 3DS, the XL's matte finish feels and looks fantastic (and doesn't get incredibly covered with fingerprints to boot). The increased screen sizes are also very appealing.
