Pax et Bonum

about

My goal is to create beautiful software that assists users in living a better life. Applications without a focus distract users from completing their tasks efficiently and hinder their ability to live a life in the real world. Although I have interests in many areas of computer science, programming, and development (as well as in life itself), my current interests focus on creating responsive and intelligent applications with an intention of simplifying (rather than increasing) the amount of work a person has to complete to get things done. Computers are very good at repeating boring or complicated tasks, so we should harness that power to make lives better.

Magically.us contains my notes, experimentations, and projects in various areas. The writings are released under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported License and the software under the MIT License, so please feel free to use them to whichever end you see fit. I urge you to consider releasing your own software or other creations under similar licenses so others can benefit from your accomplishments and the world can be a better place.

Currently, I work predominantly with the Django web framework and the Python programming language but have experience with a variety of languages and frameworks. Indeed, I moreover greatly enjoy learning new development tools and paradigms as, ultimately, I want to create wonderful experiences for users and will learn whichever languages or tools are neccessary.
